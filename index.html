FAQ
What if I don't have a team or an idea?
No worries! Once you sign up, FoodInno will assign you to a team of 5 individuals based on your skill set.
What is a hackathon?
Our hackathons are concentrated creative consulting sessions with local food companies, during which we help solve business, marketing, and design problems.
What is the challenge?
The hackathon is focused on tackling a business challenge proposed by our partnering company, House Kombucha. To hear about the actual prompt: come to our event this Sunday!
What should we bring?
Your laptop, your friends, and an innovative mind! Breakfast, lunch, and snacks will be provided.
Who can attend?
All undergraduates who are currently registered students at UC Berkeley. Bring your friends! :)
How big should teams be? Can I hack alone?
5 people per team, whether you sign up with a team or let us pair you up!
